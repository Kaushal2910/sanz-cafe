Our Story

Sanz Café began with a simple dream: to craft a space where the aroma of freshly-ground beans mingles with quiet conversation and soft instrumental music. What started as a passion project in a small corner of Nyati, Hadapsar, has grown into a beloved community hub where slow living meets great taste.
